About Me

Researcher | Energy Storage | Density Functional Theory (DFT) | Ion Batteries

I am a passionate researcher in the field of energy storage, specializing in ion batteries, hydrogen storage, and novel 2D materials. With a strong background in Density Functional Theory (DFT) calculations, I have extensively worked on BP/V3C2 MXene layered heterostructures for lithium-ion batteries (LIBs) and V2N MXene for hydrogen storage. My research focuses on discovering advanced materials that can revolutionize renewable energy solutions and energy storage technologies.
